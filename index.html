Abstract 摘要

Open-source Chinese speech resources remain dominated by single-channel, scripted, or read-aloud corpora. Large-scale, multi-channel datasets of spontaneous full-duplex dialogue—with turn overlaps, backchannels, pauses, and real-time interruptions—are still scarce. We present SmoothConv and DuplexConv: 2,100 hours of multi-party Chinese conversations in Tutoring and Social Chat, collected from natural multi-speaker settings for full-duplex interaction research.

The twin datasets pair precision with scale. SmoothConv (100h) offers expert human annotation on real multi-party conversations—transcripts with millisecond-level timestamps at segment and turn boundaries, turn-taking states, overlaps, pauses, acoustic events, and speaker gender/emotion—for fine-tuning and benchmarking. DuplexConv (2,000h) applies an LLM labeling pipeline to the same sources, yielding transcripts, turn structure, and scene-level context labels for Speech LLM pre-training at scale. Together they support end-to-end research on turn-taking, real-time interruption, and continuous spoken dialogue.

当前开源中文语音数据仍以单通道、朗读或脚本化对话为主，大规模、多通道、真实自发式全双工对话数据——涵盖话轮重叠、附和、停顿与实时打断——仍十分稀缺。我们发布 SmoothConv 与 DuplexConv 双子星数据集：同源采集 2,100 小时多方中文自然对话，覆盖教育与闲聊场景，面向全双工口语交互研究。

双子星设计兼顾标注精度与数据规模。SmoothConv（100 小时）对真实多方对话进行人工精标，涵盖分段与话轮边界的毫秒级时间戳转写、话轮状态、重叠区段、停顿、声音事件及说话人性别/情感等标签，面向全双工模型的微调与基准评测。DuplexConv（2,000 小时）对同源对话采用大模型自动标注，提供转写、话轮结构与场景语境标签，支撑 Speech LLM 大规模预训练。二者协同覆盖从精细评测到预训练的全链路研究需求。
